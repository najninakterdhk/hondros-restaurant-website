About Hondros Taverna

Since 1953, Hondros Taverna has welcomed guests with authentic Cypriot and Greek cuisine. Our mission is to provide delicious homemade food, friendly service, and a warm traditional atmosphere.

Enjoy delicious Mediterranean food with fresh ingredients, friendly staff, and a relaxing atmosphere.

Our restaurant is perfect for family dinners, birthday parties, business meetings, and romantic evenings. We always serve fresh food prepared by experienced chefs.
